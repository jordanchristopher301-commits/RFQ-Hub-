## 🎭 SCENARIO-BASED CROSS-SELL PLAYBOOKS

### SCENARIO 1: Client RFQ for PPE

**Trigger:** Customer submits RFQ for PPE/Safety Equipment  
**Time to Activate:** After quote sent + 24 hours  
**Priority:** HIGH (PPE buyers often need Power + Security)

---

#### Message Sequence A (Soft Approach)

**Day 1 - After Quote Sent:**

```
Hi [Name],

Following up on the PPE quote for [Company Name].

Quick question: Beyond the safety gear, do you have 
any of these needs?

✓ Backup power (for safety lighting, emergency systems)
✓ Site security/CCTV (to monitor PPE compliance)
✓ WiFi system (for safety alerts, incident reporting)

I can get quotes within 1 hour if interested.

[Your Name]
```

**Expected Response Rate:** 15-20%  
**If Yes:** Jump to "Pitch" below

---

#### Pitch: Power + Security Package

```
Excellent! Here's what I'd recommend:

For safety compliance, most sites need:

📦 PPE (what you quoted)
   - Helmets, gloves, respiratory
   - Total: $3,000

⚡ Backup Power
   - For emergency lighting + evacuation systems
   - Ensures safety protocols work 24/7
   - Quote: $8,000 (includes UPS + battery)

📹 Site CCTV
   - Monitor PPE compliance + site safety
   - 4K resolution, cloud backup
   - Incident evidence protection
   - Quote: $5,000 (4 cameras + recorder)

📊 BUNDLE PRICING:
   Standard (separate): $16,000
   Bundle Discount (-10%): $14,400
   Savings: $1,600! ✅

Ready to move forward?
```

---

#### Objection Handling: PPE → Power + Security

```
Objection: "I only need PPE right now"

Response:
"I totally understand. But consider this:
- Your insurance might require backup power for safety
- Site monitoring protects your investment + liability
- One vendor = easier management + loyalty discounts

What if we did PPE now, power in Q3?"

---

Objection: "Price is too high for all three"

Response:
"Fair point. Let's prioritize:
1. Essential: PPE ($3,000) + Power ($8,000) = $11,000
2. Add later: Security ($5,000) when budget allows

Or I can reduce scope on one category to hit your budget?"

---

Objection: "We don't have budget for power/security"

Response:
"No problem! Here's what we can do:
- Quote it all now
- You have 30 days to decide
- Get approval from your boss with pricing
- Then we fast-track delivery

Think of it as planning ahead?"
```

## ⚠️ CROSS-SELL GUARDRAILS

**To maintain customer trust:**

```
✅ DO:
   - Lead with customer value (not vendor revenue)
   - Offer genuine solutions (not forced bundles)
   - Respect customer budget constraints
   - Provide realistic pricing with no surprises
   - Follow up appropriately (not aggressively)
   - Honor customer "no thank you" responses

❌ DON'T:
   - Upsell products they don't need
   - Pressure customers into bundles
   - Surprise pricing on cross-sell
   - Contact too frequently
   - Oversell (commit what you can't deliver)
   - Ignore customers who say "no"
```
