# 🚀 RFQ-HUB: COMPLETE B2B PROCUREMENT SYSTEM FOR GUYANA

## 📊 SYSTEM OVERVIEW

A complete, production-ready RFQ (Request for Quotation) capture and management system for businesses operating in Guyana. Designed to convert leads into deals at scale, with emphasis on **speed** (<15 min response), **breadth** (6+ product categories), and **cross-selling** (2-3x revenue per customer).

**Status:** ✅ All 5 core modules deployed and ready for implementation

### ✅ MODULE 2: WhatsApp RFQ Capture System
**File:** `2-WHATSAPP-SCRIPTS.md`

15+ copy-paste scripts for converting cold prospects into RFQ submissions
```
├─ 4 industry-specific entry scripts (Oil & Gas, Construction, Government, Utilities)
├─ 5 category-specific trigger messages (PPE, Power, IT, Security, Marine)
├─ 5 cross-sell trigger sequences (PPE→Power+Security, IT→Power+Security, etc.)
├─ 4-step follow-up sequence (Day 1, 3, 6, 9)
├─ 6 objection handlers (already have supplier, price too high, etc.)
├─ Daily outreach schedule (20 messages/day broken into time blocks)
└─ Performance tracking template
```

**Use This To:**
- Send 20 personalized WhatsApp messages daily
- Follow up systematically (no contacts fall through cracks)
- Handle objections confidently
- Convert conversations into formal RFQs

### ✅ MODULE 3: RFQ Intake System
**File:** `3-RFQ-INTAKE-FORMS.md`

26-field Google Form that captures all info needed for rapid quoting
```
├─ Contact info section (name, company, industry, job title)
├─ Product category & specifics (what they need, quantity, budget)
├─ Delivery & timeline (ASAP vs 60 days, delivery location)
├─ Commercial terms (payment terms, PO required, existing customer)
├─ Urgency & decision factors (how urgent, who approves, competing quotes)
├─ File uploads (RFQ documents, technical specs, drawings)
├─ Additional info (referral source, preferred contact method)
├─ Zapier automation workflow (auto-route to suppliers, send confirmations)
├─ Email templates (immediate confirmation, quote attached)
└─ Form analytics & optimization guide
```

**Use This To:**
- Convert WhatsApp conversations into trackable RFQs
- Automate quote requests to suppliers
- Send instant confirmations to customers
- Never lose a lead due to disorganization

   ✅ Fix: Personalize every message with company research

   ✅ Fix: Use the 4-step follow-up sequence (Day 1, 3, 6, 9)
